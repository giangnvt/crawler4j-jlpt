She was about to know about my secret plan, so I told a lie at once.


言い過ぎた、とみんながとっさにそう思った。
Everyone knew instantly he’d gone too far.

His quick brain was working.


「そんなことないよ。」彼はとっさに答えた。
“Of course not,” he said quickly.
